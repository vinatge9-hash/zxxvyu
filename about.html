Our Story

Niyantri Cafe began as a small, community-driven coffee spot with a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. We source beans from sustainable farms and roast in small batches to preserve flavor and aroma. Our space is designed for conversation, creativity, and connection.

From the first pour to the last sip, we aim to create a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home. Welcome to the family.
